About Me

Suleiman Adam Gbale is a passionate teacher, writer, and researcher dedicated to impacting lives through education and Islamic guidance. He has authored several inspiring books and continues to guide young minds toward purposeful living.

Background

I run Abu Zayd Institute, where we teach Qur’ān memorization, Tajwīd, and personal development for students. I am currently pursuing a degree in Islamic Studies at Kwara State University, Malete.

Vision

To build a generation of students who are both academically competent and spiritually grounded — leaders who carry Islamic values into every field of life.
